Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hair color for Cyprus climate?

For Cyprus's sunny climate, we recommend hair colors with UV protection. Lighter shades like caramel and honey blonde work well, as they naturally lighten in the sun without looking damaged.

How often should I get a haircut?

We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy. For longer hair, every 10-12 weeks is sufficient.
